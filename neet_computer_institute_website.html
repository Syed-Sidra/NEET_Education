FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

CCC (Course on Computer Concepts) is a 3-month basic computer literacy course ideal for government job eligibility. O Level is a 1-year comprehensive IT diploma equivalent to a foundation degree — it's more advanced and covers programming, databases, and web design. Both are NIELIT/government certified.
Yes! Our CCC and O Level certificates from NIELIT are mandatory and recognized for UP State Government jobs, Central Government recruitment, and bank jobs. The certificates are issued directly by NIELIT — a Government of India body under Ministry of Electronics & IT.
Absolutely! We provide 100% job assistance through our dedicated placement cell. We have partnerships with 1200+ hiring organizations including IT companies, CA firms, government departments, design agencies, and banks. We also conduct mock interviews, CV building sessions, and campus drives.
We maintain small batch sizes of maximum 20 students for personalized attention. Batches are available in morning (8–11 AM), afternoon (12–3 PM), and evening (4–7 PM) slots — Monday to Saturday, so you can choose as per your convenience.
Yes! We offer flexible fee payment in 2–4 installments for all courses. Merit-based scholarships of up to 50% fee waiver are also available. SC/ST/OBC students can avail additional government scholarship benefits. Please visit the institute or call us for fee details.
