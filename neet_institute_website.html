About Us

Nurturing Excellence Since 2016

NEET Institute of Technology and Management is a registered Society / Educational & Welfare Institute located in Nagarika Enclave, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. We are dedicated to providing world-class coaching with a student-first philosophy.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower every student with quality education, expert mentorship, and the tools needed to crack competitive exams and build successful careers.

🌟 Our Vision

To become Uttar Pradesh's most trusted educational institution, producing top rankers and future leaders who contribute to national development.
